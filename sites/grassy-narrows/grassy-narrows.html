Introduction to the Issue

The community of Grassy Narrows is a prime example of environmental injustice. Like many other native communities, Grassy Narrows, located in Kenora, Ontario, has suffered from colonialist ideals and has lost several cultural aspects as a result. This webpage will highlight the environmental hardships that Grassy Narrows has had to deal with in the past, as well as in the present and will detail the effects that these issues have had on the community and on their culture. At the bottom, you'll notice that I included a section dedicated to activism. This is where I'll talk about what Grassy Narrows is doing as a community to resist environmental injustice as well as how they are collaborating with non-natives to make their voices heard by those in power.
